Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 2026

Hestia Care (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the Hestia Care mobile application and related services.

Authentication and Account Information

Hestia Care allows users to create and access an account using email and password authentication only.

When you sign up or log in, we collect:

  • Email address
  • A unique account identifier

Passwords are securely encrypted and are never stored in plain text.

How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate users
  • Provide access to app features
  • Maintain security and prevent unauthorized access
  • Support account recovery and user support requests

The app does not use social login providers such as Google, Facebook, or Apple.

Data Sharing

Hestia Care does not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

Personal data is not shared with third parties, except where necessary to operate the application securely through our platform infrastructure and solely for core app functionality.

Account Deletion

Users may delete their account at any time directly from within the app settings.

When an account is deleted:

  • The user account is permanently removed
  • All associated personal data is deleted from our systems

This is subject only to any legal or regulatory obligations that may require data retention.

Data Security

We take reasonable and appropriate measures to protect personal information, including encryption and secure storage practices, to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Children’s Privacy

Hestia Care is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
